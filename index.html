Create Instant Polls

Poll.Pizza allows you to instantly create disposable short-term-use polls. Simply create a new question with two to four options and you're ready to share the voting link via your unique url!

Voting can be anonymous or you can specify a name, then results are publicly displayed on a pizza chart.

When a poll with the same url is created, it takes the previous poll's place. Have fun!
